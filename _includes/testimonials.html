The bike project has allowed me to maintain a fleet of bikes for a family of 5 and affordably upgrade my daughter’s bikes as they outgrow them annually. But the real fun has been learning the practical, mechanical skills of bike maintenance along the way. In a country that’s moving toward machines with “no user-serviceable parts inside” and treats consumer goods as disposable, the bike project is a welcome harbor for those who like to tinker and see value in used things.- Seth Brown
